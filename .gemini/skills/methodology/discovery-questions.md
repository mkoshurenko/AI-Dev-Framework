# Discovery Questions (Analyst & Designer)

## Core Requirement Questions
1. What problem are we solving?
2. Who is the primary user for this feature?
3. What does success look like?
4. What's the minimum viable version?

## Data & Behavior Questions
1. What data is needed and where does it come from?
2. What are the happy paths?
3. What could go wrong? (Errors, edge cases).
4. Any specific validations?

## UI/UX Specific Questions
1. Where does this fit in the app navigation?
2. Are there existing similar patterns?
3. Accessibility requirements (colors, touch targets, screen readers)?
4. Platform-specific needs (Android vs. iOS)?
